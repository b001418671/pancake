Powdered Sugar Pancakes

Soft, fluffy pancakes dusted with a delicate layer of powdered sugar offer a light and sweet treat. The airy texture combined with the subtle sweetness of the sugar makes each bite a simple yet delightful indulgence, perfect for any breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Melted butter or vegetable oil
  • Vanilla extract
  • Powdered sugar (for dusting)
